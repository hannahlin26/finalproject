  • Ingredients
    1. 1 pound of ice cubes
    2. 2 tablespoons sweet bean paste (or more to taste)
    3. 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk (or more to taste)
    4. 16 mini sweet rice cakes(mochi)
    5. 80g strawberry, chopped
    6. 45g kiwi, thinly sliced
    7. 45g blueberry
    8. 45g pineapple, chopped
    10. 1 scoop of ice cream of your choice (optional)
  • Instructions
    1. Put the ice blocks into a food processor (or shaved ice machine if you have one). Grind the ice (for about 20 seconds) until it has a smooth texture.
    2. Place the shaved ice into a chilled serving bowl. Top up with your choice of fruit, red bean paste, mochi. Garnish with sweetened condensed milk.
    3. Serve
