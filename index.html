North America

North American cuisine includes foods native to or popular in North America, such as Canadian cuisine, American cuisine, Mexican cuisine and Central American cuisine.

South American

South American cuisine has many influences, due to the ethnic fusion of South America. The most characteristic are Native American, African, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, and Indian-South Asian.

Asian

Asian cuisine includes several major regional cuisines: East Asian, Southeast Asian, South Asian, Central Asian, and Middle Eastern/Western Asian.

European

European cuisine consists of food from Central, Western, Southern and Eastern Europe. Most popular include Italian, Greek, and French.

Africa

Includes cuisines from throughout the continent's vast populations: Central Africa, East Africa, North Africa, Southern Africa and West Africa.
