    Ingredients
    1. 1 box (1 lb) penne pasta
    2. 3 tablespoons butter
    3. 2 teaspoons minced garlic
    4. 3 tablespoons flour
    5. 1 cup chicken broth
    6. 1 cup milk
    7. 2 teaspoons dried parsley
    8. 1/2 cup gratede parmesan cheese
    9. salt and pepper to taste
    Instructions
    1. 1. Cook pasta accordinng to packaged directions.

    2. 2. Melt butter in a medium sauce pan, add garlic

    3. 3. Cook for 1 minute over medium head

    4. 4. Add flour amd cook for an additional minute, stirring constantly

    5. 5. Add parsley and parmesan cheese. Add desired salt and pepper. Continue stirring until cheese has melted. Serve immediately

    6. 6. Pour sauce over pasta and serve
